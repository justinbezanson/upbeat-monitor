# UptimeRobot Pricing Tiers and Features

## Free Plan
*   **Price:** Free forever
*   **Monitors:** 50
*   **Check Interval:** 5 minutes
*   **Features:**
    *   Monitor types: HTTP(s), Ping, Port, Keyword
    *   1 Status Page
    *   Email notifications
    *   Mobile App access

## Solo Plan
*   **Price:** Starts at ~$7/month
*   **Monitors:** 10
*   **Check Interval:** 1 minute
*   **Features:**
    *   Includes Free features plus:
    *   SSL & Domain expiration monitoring
    *   Advanced notifications (SMS/Voice alerts supported)
    *   Basic Status Pages
    *   Limited Integrations (~8-9)

## Team Plan
*   **Price:** Starts at ~$29/month
*   **Monitors:** 100
*   **Check Interval:** 1 minute
*   **Features:**
    *   Includes Solo features plus:
    *   Full-featured Status Pages (White-labeling, Password protection)
    *   All Integrations (~12+)
    *   3 Login Seats & 3 Notify Seats
    *   Maintenance Windows

## Enterprise Plan
*   **Price:** Starts at ~$54/month (scaling with monitors)
*   **Monitors:** 200+
*   **Check Interval:** 30 seconds
*   **Features:**
    *   Includes Team features plus:
    *   Unlimited Status Pages
    *   5 Login Seats & 5 Notify Seats
    *   Priority Support
